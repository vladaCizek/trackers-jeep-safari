                                                                                                                                                                                  Jeep Safari on Cyprus

                                                                                                                                                                                  “Our aim is to leave you speechless and turn you into a story teller”

                                                                                                                                                                                  If you are seeking adventure, fun and an exciting way to see the Island you have come to the right place. Our tours are designed with you in mind and are not there to tick things off the brochure or collect half- hearted souvenirs to be stored in dusty shelves of your spare rooms. Rather we’ll take you to find the best of the Islands culture, traditions, travelling on off the beat dirt track roads into country sites and untouched nature, tiny uninhibited villages and breath-taking viewpoints where you will sit back and dismiss the passing time, content to be spent and consumed. With us you will feel like a traveler not a tourist. You will try new things, meet new people, and look beyond what’s right in front of you. This is the key and a guarantee to each one of our excursions.
