                                                                                                                                                                                  Once Upon A Time Mini Jeep Safari

                                                                                                                                                                                  undefined

                                                                                                                                                                                  “Step back in time adventure.”

                                                                                                                                                                                  A half day, fun packed and enlightening day out for the young and young at heart.

                                                                                                                                                                                  We begin our journey through the red villages passing through the fields of “Taro and Potato’s” which these areas are famous for until we reach an almost secluded reservoir where rare migratory birds nest with the opportunity to toss a stone or two in this breath taking dam.

                                                                                                                                                                                  Our adventure continues to a very special park where we step back in time and observe how a traditional Cypriot village functioned once upon a time, where the children can play carelessly and interact with various farm animals, (including Cyprus Donkeys of course), wonder around the small orchard or play in the kids playground while the more cultured of us can explore inside these houses and church of this reconstructed village or simply sit back and take it all in sitting by the many water fountains enjoying a Cypriot coffee.

                                                                                                                                                                                  We then head off to a secluded traditional Cypriot fishing harbor taking in the abundant of colour of the fishing boats moored in the river, their very exceptional jetties and a couple of traditional fresh fish taverns basking in the sun on the shore where the river meets the sea.

                                                                                                                                                                                  Our final destination is a nearby private sandy beach where you will be given free time for a swim.

                                                                                                                                                                                  Program may alter slightly during the winter months between November till March.
